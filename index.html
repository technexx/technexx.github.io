About Me:

Hi. I'm in Android developer from Los Angeles, California. I have been independently coding since 2020, with 4 published apps on the Google Play Store.

I am proficient with Java, Kotlin, XML, Jetpack Compose, Coroutines, SQLite/Room, RESTful APIs, and Git. I am also very familiar with the Android Studio ecosystem, including Gradle its various SDKs.

My most recent app is an integrated timer and calorie tracker named Burn, Track, Repeat. It allows the user to set timers for over 350 unique activities, from weightlifting to cycling, and automatically counts the calories they burn based on their height, weight, age and gender. Users can also enter their caloric intake, and compare the total difference for weight gain/loss over time.

My previous apps include a cuisine/restaurant randomizer, a pet adoption searcher for the Los Angeles County shelter system, and a text-based game wherein one tries to build a world-conquering creature in their basement.

As a self-taught, independent developer, I'm able to effectively manage my time and complete tasks without supervision. As a former barista, I have a solid foundation of interpersonal skills, customer insight, and conflict resolution. I'm eager to tackle new challenges, expand my skill base, and contribute in meaningful way to worthwhile projects.
