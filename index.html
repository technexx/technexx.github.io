About Me:

Hi. I'm in Android developer from Los Angeles, California. I have been independently coding since 2019, and have published six apps on the Google Play Store.

I am proficient with Java, Kotlin, XML, Jetpack Compose, and SQLite. I am experienced with Android's Room library, in addition to its ViewModel and LiveData architectural components. I am also familiar with coroutines and web scraping/data parsing.

My most recent app is an integrated timer and calorie tracker named Burn, Track, Repeat. It allows the user to set timers for over 350 unique activities, from weightlifting to cycling, and automatically counts the calories they burn based on their height, weight, age and gender. Users can also enter their caloric intake, and compare the total difference and weight gained/loss over time.

My previous apps include one that searches for and returns all animals within the Los Angeles County shelter system, a "pro versus con" list creator with weighted values and optional decision maker, and a text-based game wherein one tries to build a world-conquering creature in their basement.

As a self-taught, independent developer, I'm able to effectively manage my time and complete tasks without supervision. As a former barista, I have a solid foundation of interpersonal skills, customer insight, and conflict resolution. I'm eager to tackle new challenges, expand my skill base, and contribute in meaningful way to worthwhile projects.
